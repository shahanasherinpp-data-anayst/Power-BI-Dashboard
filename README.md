📊 Amazon Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

🧾 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing Amazon India’s 2025 sales data to uncover meaningful insights into product performance, customer behavior, and regional trends.
Using Microsoft Power BI, the dashboard transforms raw e-commerce data into interactive visualizations and actionable business insights to support better decision-making.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze overall sales performance
Identify top-performing product categories
Understand monthly sales trends
Evaluate delivery performance and return rates
Discover regional sales patterns
Provide insights for business optimization

📂 Dataset Information
Total Records: 15,000
Total Features: 6 columns
Year: 2025
Domain: E-commerce (Amazon India)

📌 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
💰 Total Sales: ₹1.12 Billion
📦 Total Orders: 15,000
🛒 Total Quantity Sold: 44,770
🔁 Return Rate: 32.54%

📊 Dashboard Insights:
🛍️ Sales by Category:
Top categories: Beauty, Electronics, Books
Contribute 60%+ of total revenue
Lower performance: Clothing, Home & Kitchen

📅 Monthly Sales Trends:
🔝 Peak Months: May, August, December
🔻 Low Months: February, June
Indicates seasonal demand and promotion impact

🚚 Delivery Status:
Delivered: 5.08K
Returned: 4.88K
Pending: 5.04K
⚠️ High return and pending rates highlight operational challenges
